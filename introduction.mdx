## What is Lightning AI?

Lightning AI is a unified cloud platform designed for AI teams. It removes infrastructure complexity so researchers and engineers can focus entirely on building models and products. From interactive development in Studio to large-scale distributed training, everything runs on the same platform under a single login.

## Platform Overview

Lightning AI is organized around three core primitives:

- **Studio** — a cloud IDE for interactive development, notebooks, and experiments.
- **Training** — scalable compute for pretraining and fine-tuning large models.
- **Teams** — organizations, teamspaces, and role-based access to keep collaboration structured.
