## Startup Plan Overview

The Lightning AI Startup plan is designed for small, fast-moving teams. It includes:

- Up to **10 members** across unlimited Teamspaces.
- A monthly credit allocation for compute (GPUs, CPUs, storage).
- Access to all Studio features and standard training compute tiers.
- Community support with a 48-hour SLA.

Upgrade paths to the Enterprise tier are available at any time without data migration.

## Recommended Onboarding Steps

Follow this sequence to get your team productive on day one:

1. **Create your Organization** — one owner signs up and sets the org name and slug.
2. **Invite your team** — send invitations from **Settings → Members** with appropriate roles.
3. **Create a shared Teamspace** — name it after your first project or `main`.
4. **Connect a data source** — mount an S3 bucket or GCS bucket via **Teamspace → Storage**.
5. **Launch a Studio** — verify the environment and install project dependencies.

## Applying for Startup Credits

Qualifying startups (incorporated, fewer than 5 years old, under $5M raised) can apply for additional compute credits through the Lightning AI for Startups program. To apply:

1. Navigate to **Settings → Billing → Apply for Startup Credits**.
2. Submit your company details and a brief description of your project.
3. The team reviews applications within 5 business days.
