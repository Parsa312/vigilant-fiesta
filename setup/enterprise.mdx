## Enterprise plan features

The Enterprise tier adds capabilities required by larger organizations and regulated industries:

- **Unlimited members** with granular RBAC.
- **SSO enforcement** via SAML 2.0 or OIDC.
- **Private networking** — deploy into your own VPC (AWS, GCP, Azure).
- **Audit logs** — exportable event history for compliance and security reviews.
- **Dedicated support** with a named CSM and a 4-hour SLA for critical issues.
- **Custom contracts** — annual invoicing, BAAs, DPAs, and MSAs available.

## Configure Single Sign-On

To configure SSO for your Organization:

1. Go to **Settings → Security → Single Sign-On**.
2. Select your identity provider type (SAML 2.0 or OIDC).
3. Enter the metadata URL or manually paste the IdP certificate and endpoints.
4. Map IdP groups to Lightning AI Organization and Teamspace roles.
5. Test the connection with a pilot user before enforcing SSO for all members.

Once SSO is enforced, password-based login is disabled for all non-owner accounts.

## Private VPC deployment

For teams with data residency or network isolation requirements, Lightning AI can be deployed into a customer-managed VPC:

1. Provide your cloud account ID and preferred region to your Lightning AI Solutions Engineer.
2. Lightning AI provisions a dedicated control plane and compute cluster in your VPC.
3. All data remains within your network boundary; no data crosses the public internet.
4. Egress charges from your cloud provider apply at standard rates.

Contact [enterprise@lightning.ai](mailto:enterprise@lightning.ai) to begin the VPC onboarding process.
