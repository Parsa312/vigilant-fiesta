## Member Roles

Lightning AI uses a two-level role system — one at the Organization level and one at the Teamspace level.

| Role | Scope | Capabilities |
|---|---|---|
| **Owner** | Organization | Full admin: billing, member management, all Teamspaces |
| **Admin** | Organization | Manage members and Teamspaces; cannot change billing |
| **Member** | Organization | Access only the Teamspaces they are explicitly invited to |
| **Admin** | Teamspace | Manage Teamspace settings and members |
| **Editor** | Teamspace | Create and modify Studios and Jobs |
| **Viewer** | Teamspace | Read-only access to artifacts and logs |

## Inviting and Removing Members

To invite someone to your Organization:

1. Go to **Settings → Members → Invite Member**.
2. Enter their email address and select an Organization role.
3. Optionally add them to one or more Teamspaces at the same time.

The invitee receives an email with a sign-up or login link. To remove a member, click the overflow menu next to their name and select **Remove**.

## Single Sign-On (SSO)

Enterprise organizations can enforce SSO via SAML 2.0 or OIDC. Once SSO is enabled:

- All members must authenticate through the configured identity provider.
- Just-in-time (JIT) provisioning can automatically create accounts for new SSO users.
- Refer to the [Enterprise Setup](/setup/enterprise) guide for configuration steps.
