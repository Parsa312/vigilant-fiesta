Organizations are the top-level structure for managing AI work on Lightning. An organization groups your members, cloud accounts, and billing under one account, and is subdivided into Teamspaces for different projects or use cases. Members join with a role — Admin, Member, or Guest — that determines what they can access and create.

## What Is an Organization?

An Organization is the root entity in Lightning AI. It maps to a company or a team that shares a billing account and a pool of compute credits. Every user belongs to at least one organization, and all Teamspaces, Studios, and Jobs live inside an organization.

## Creating and Managing an Organization

Organizations are created at sign-up or via **Settings → Organizations → New Organization**. As an owner you can:

- Set a display name and slug used in URLs.
- Attach a payment method and view credit usage.
- Invite members and assign roles (see [Members](/concepts/members)).
- Create and archive [Teamspaces](/concepts/teamspaces) within the org.

## Billing and Quotas

Compute costs accrue at the organization level regardless of which Teamspace or member triggered them. Owners can:

- View a live credit balance in **Settings → Billing**.
- Set spending alerts to notify admins when usage crosses a threshold.
- Request quota increases for GPUs and storage through the support portal.
