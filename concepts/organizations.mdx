Organizations are the top-level structure for managing AI work on Lightning AI. An organization groups your members, cloud accounts, and billing under one account, and is subdivided into Teamspaces for different projects or use cases. Members join with a role — Admin, Member, or Guest — that determines what they can access and create.

## What Is an Organization?

An Organization is the root entity in Lightning AI. It maps to a company or a team that shares a billing account and a pool of compute credits. Every user belongs to at least one organization, and all Teamspaces, Studios, and Jobs live inside an organization.

## Creating and Managing an Organization

You create an organization when you create your account. To create a new organization:

1. Select your organization name in the top-left navigation.
2. Select **New organization**.

As the owner of the organization, you can:

- Attach a payment method and view credit usage.
- Invite members and assign roles. For more information, see [Members](/concepts/members).
- Create and archive [Teamspaces](/concepts/teamspaces) within the organization.

## Billing and Quotas

Compute costs accrue at the organization level regardless of which Teamspace or member triggered them. Owners can:

- Add a payment method and credits.
- Set alerts when an organization balance falls below a certain amount.
