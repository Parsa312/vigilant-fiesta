JupyterLab is available in every Studio directly from the browser — no local installation needed. Studios start on a free CPU instance by default, and you can upgrade to a GPU at any time without losing your environment.

## Steps

1. Open or create a Studio. If you don't have one yet, see [Create a Studio](/quickstart/studio).

2. Select **JupyterLab** on the right toolbar. JupyterLab opens in a new browser tab connected to your Studio's environment.

3. Create or upload a notebook and start working.
