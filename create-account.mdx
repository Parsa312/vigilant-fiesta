## Step 1: Create a free account

Go to [lightning.ai](https://lightning.ai) and click **Start free**. No credit card is required. All accounts start on the free tier, which includes SSH access, any IDE, and unlimited background execution.

<Tip>Use a work or university email for instant verification.</Tip>

## Step 2: Start a Studio

Navigate to [studio.lightning.ai](https://studio.lightning.ai) or click **New Studio** from the dashboard. The Studio starts on a free CPU instance. Install your dependencies here, then switch to a GPU when you're ready to run your workload.

## Step 3: Start building

From your Studio you can train models, run inference, build agents, and deploy APIs. See the following guides to continue:

- [Quickstart](/quickstart)
- [Pretrain a model](/training/pretrain)
- [Configure your Studio](/studio/configure)
